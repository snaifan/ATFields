Tomato Mastery Growing Tips

Learn to grow vibrant MoneyMaker tomatoes with tips from my backyard farm.

1. Soil is everything 🌱

Healthy tomatoes start with healthy soil. Make sure it’s rich in compost, well-drained, and loose so roots can grow deep.

2. Pruning & support ✂️🪢

Remove lower leaves that touch the soil and prune unnecessary suckers so the plant puts more energy into fruits instead of excess leaves.

Use stakes, cages, or strings to support plants for better air circulation and sunlight.

3. Consistent feeding & watering 💧🍵

Avoid irregular watering – it causes fruit cracking and blossom end rot.

Feed the soil with the right food and drinks, every few weeks and keep watering steady, not drought then flood.
